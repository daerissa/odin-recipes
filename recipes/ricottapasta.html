Simple pasta dish with spinat and ricotta cheese

Description

Quick and easy pasta dishes are a blessing on a busy day. One of the ingredients in this tasteful recipe is the ricotta cheese. Together with spinach, tomatoes, and pasta the dish is simple yet delicious.

Ingredients

  • 400g tortellini
  • neutral oil
  • 1 small red onion
  • 3 boats of garlic
  • 250g spinach
  • 250g ricotta
  • 1/2-2dl water (or water from pasta
  • 10-12 cherry tomatoes
  • grated nutmeg
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package.Pour off the water, but save approx. 1 dl of the pasta water.
  2. Use a deep pan or saucepan and fry the red onion and garlic in a little neutral oil until the onion is shiny and soft, but without taking on color.
  3. Clean the spinach and remove thick stems and ugly leaves. Rinse well and shake off most of the water. Roughly chop the spinach and put it in the pot together with the onion.If necessary, add a little more oil and let the spinach simmer while stirring, until it just collapses.
  4. tir together the ricotta and some of the pasta water (milk / cream), and stir it together with the vegetables.
  5. Season with grated nutmeg, salt and pepper. Add the tomatoes and let the sauce simmer for a few minutes, until the tomatoes are thoroughly warm.
  6. Serve the pasta with a generous scoop of spinach and ricotta sauce, or mix together pasta and sauce, if you prefer. Give the dish a few strokes with the pepper grinder and feel free to serve good bread as an accompaniment.

Enjoy!
