Oven-baked salmon with spinach, broccoli and pomegranate

Description

A delicious all-in-one form dish using salmon, vegetables and sauce, with pomegranate seeds. Pleasing for taste as well as eyes.

Ingredient

  • 500g salmon
  • 100g spinach
  • 1 small broccoli in small pieces
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • salt and pepper
  • nutmeg
  • 2-3dl whipped cream or creme fraiche
  • 1/2 pomegranate
  • potatose or other side dish

Steps

  1. Grease a large baking tin with olive oil and spread the spinach, broccoli bouquets and garlic in the tin. Season with salt, pepper and a little nutmeg.
  2. Add the cream to the dish (or spread sour cream on top) and then place the salmon fillet on top and add a little salt and pepper on top.
  3. Scrape the kernels out of the pomegranate and spread them over the fish, before serving. Dishc can be served with rice, potatoes or others according to your taste.

Enjoy!
